🩺 Cause Reference Guide
🦠
Disease
Bacterial/fungal infection. Isolate affected batch, treat with approved medication. Log medication as an expense.
💧
Low Oxygen
DO below 4 mg/L is critical. Increase aeration immediately. Reduce feed. Log water quality after correction.
🌡
Temperature Stress
Above 34°C or below 15°C causes stress. Shade pond, adjust feed rate. Monitor temp daily.
🦅
Predator
Birds, snakes, or other animals. Install nets over the pond. Check pond perimeter fencing.
❓
Unknown
Investigate water quality, check for disease signs. Collect sample for lab testing if deaths exceed 50.
🚨
Alert Threshold
Logging >50 deaths in a single event triggers an automatic critical alert on the dashboard.
